What we're building

Where conviction is going to work

ZAMP backs companies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, autonomy, defense, and energy, the infrastructure of the next decade. Here's where that conviction is focused today.

my_location

Positioning & robotics

Merlin TPS: Positioning where GPS can't reach

Merlin's Terrestrial Positioning System keeps delivery robots, drones, and autonomous vehicles connected where GPS fails, inside dense urban cores and steel-and-concrete buildings. ZAMP is helping Merlin move from hardware to a pure-software platform that can scale across the entire autonomy industry.

"Our first venture investment, and a model for how we roll up our sleeves to help companies scale."

security

Defense technology

Atreyd: Autonomous defense, onshore

Atreyd builds AI-guided autonomous drone systems, technology already in active use by allied militaries. ZAMP is helping scale U.S. manufacturing, with a new facility near Miami bringing production onshore.

"Autonomy and AI are redefining how nations are defended, one of our highest-conviction themes."

gavel

AI & automation

Evidencia: AI-powered dispute resolution

Evidencia uses AI and automation to streamline the dispute-resolution process. ZAMP is helping the team scale from Spain into new markets across South America and the United States.

"Discovered at the Mobile World Congress, a natural fit for our hands-on, consultative model."

dns

AI cloud infrastructure

Nexus Core Systems: The compute backbone for European AI

Nexus Core Systems is building a European "neo-cloud", sovereign, cost-efficient AI infrastructure powered by some of the cleanest, lowest-cost energy on the continent. Led by a former NVIDIA EMEA sales leader, the team is assembling a pipeline of hyperscaler and neo-cloud partners.

"AI runs on compute and energy. Nexus sits at the infrastructure layer we believe is the foundation of the next decade."

Frontier venture

The Frontier Fund: backing what comes next

Beyond its flagship positions, ZAMP backs early-stage startups across artificial intelligence, defense and military technology, and clean energy. The team works a focused funnel of companies, applying one consistent test: something unique and differentiated, strong people, a compelling story, and a big market.

"We back founders building what comes next, and we are not passive capital."
