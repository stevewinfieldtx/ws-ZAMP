## Why the split (the honest reason)
Posting raise targets, valuations, SPAC timing, and return projections on a public, ungated site can trigger securities-compliance issues, general solicitation and forward-looking/return claims regulators scrutinize closely. Keeping the public page qualitative and gating the numbers protects ZAMP while still giving real investors everything they need. (Not legal advice, worth a quick check with ZAMP's counsel.)
